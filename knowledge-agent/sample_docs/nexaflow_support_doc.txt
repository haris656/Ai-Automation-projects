SECTION 1: GETTING STARTED
================================================================

1.1 What is NexaFlow?
----------------------
NexaFlow is a workflow automation platform that helps businesses
automate repetitive tasks, connect their tools, and build
intelligent pipelines without writing code. It supports over
500 integrations and includes built-in AI capabilities for
data processing, content generation, and decision automation.

1.2 Creating Your Account
--------------------------
To create a NexaFlow account:
1. Visit app.nexaflow.io and click "Start Free Trial"
2. Enter your work email address
3. Verify your email via the confirmation link sent to your inbox
4. Complete your company profile
5. Choose your plan (Starter, Professional, or Enterprise)

Your free trial includes 14 days of full Professional plan access
with no credit card required.

1.3 System Requirements
------------------------
NexaFlow is a fully cloud-based platform accessible via any
modern web browser. We recommend:
- Chrome 110+ or Firefox 112+ for best performance
- Stable internet connection (minimum 5 Mbps)
- Screen resolution of 1280x720 or higher

No software installation is required.

================================================================

Starter Plan — $29/month

Professional Plan — $79/month

Enterprise Plan — Custom pricing
  - Unlimited everything

  - SLA guarantees (99.9% uptime)
  - SSO and advanced security controls
  - Custom integrations
  - Unlimited team members
  - On-premise deployment option

2.2 Billing
------------
All plans are billed monthly or annually. Annual billing provides
a 20% discount compared to monthly billing. Invoices are generated
on the first of each month and sent to your billing email address.

Accepted payment methods: Visa, Mastercard, American Express,
PayPal, and bank transfer (Enterprise only).

2.3 Upgrading or Downgrading
------------------------------
You can upgrade your plan at any time from Settings > Billing.
Upgrades take effect immediately and you are charged a prorated
amount for the remainder of the billing cycle.

Downgrades take effect at the start of the next billing cycle.
If your current usage exceeds the limits of the lower plan,
workflows will be paused until usage is within the new limits.

2.4 Cancellation Policy
------------------------
You can cancel your subscription at any time from Settings >
Billing > Cancel Subscription. Your account remains active until
the end of the current billing period. We do not offer refunds
for partial months.

After cancellation, your data is retained for 30 days before
being permanently deleted. You can export your data at any time
from Settings > Data Export.

SECTION 3: WORKFLOWS
================================================================

3.1 Creating a Workflow
------------------------
To create a new workflow:
1. Click the "New Workflow" button from the dashboard
2. Choose to start from scratch or use a template
3. Add a trigger (the event that starts your workflow)
4. Add actions (the steps your workflow performs)
5. Test your workflow using the built-in testing tool
6. Activate the workflow when ready

3.2 Triggers
-------------
NexaFlow supports the following trigger types:
- Schedule: Run on a time-based schedule (e.g., every day at 9 AM)
- Webhook: Triggered by an HTTP POST request from an external service
- Event: Triggered by an event in a connected integration
  (e.g., new email received, new row in spreadsheet)
- Manual: Triggered manually from the dashboard

3.3 Actions
------------
Actions are the steps your workflow performs after the trigger fires.
Common action types include:
- Send email or SMS
- Create or update a record in a CRM
- Post to Slack or Teams
- Make an HTTP request to any API
- Run AI processing on text or data
- Filter, transform, or route data
- Delay execution for a set period

3.4 Error Handling
-------------------
NexaFlow automatically retries failed actions up to 3 times with
exponential backoff. If a workflow fails after all retries, you
will receive an email notification with details of the failure.

You can configure custom error handling from the workflow settings,
including routing failed executions to a separate error workflow.

3.5 Execution Limits
---------------------
Task executions are counted per action step. A workflow with 5
actions that runs 100 times uses 500 task executions. Executions
reset on the first of each month.

If you exceed your monthly limit, workflows will pause until the
next month or until you upgrade your plan. You can purchase
additional executions from Settings > Billing > Add-ons.

SECTION 4: INTEGRATIONS
================================================================

4.1 Available Integrations
---------------------------
NexaFlow connects with over 500 tools and platforms including:
- CRM: Salesforce, HubSpot, Zoho CRM, Pipedrive
- Communication: Gmail, Outlook, Slack, Microsoft Teams, WhatsApp
- Databases: MySQL, PostgreSQL, Airtable, Google Sheets
- E-commerce: Shopify, WooCommerce, Stripe
- Project Management: Asana, Jira, Trello, Monday.com
- AI: OpenAI, Anthropic, Google Gemini, Hugging Face
- Cloud: AWS, Google Cloud, Azure, Dropbox

4.2 Connecting an Integration
-------------------------------
To connect a new integration:
1. Go to Settings > Integrations
2. Search for the integration you want to connect
3. Click "Connect" and follow the authentication steps
4. The integration will appear as available in your workflows

Most integrations use OAuth 2.0 for secure authentication.
Your credentials are encrypted at rest and never stored in plain text.

4.3 Custom Integrations
------------------------
Professional and Enterprise users can connect any tool using our
HTTP Request action, which supports all REST API methods (GET,
POST, PUT, DELETE, PATCH) with custom headers, authentication,
and request bodies.

Enterprise users can also request custom integration development
through their dedicated support manager.

SECTION 5: AI FEATURES
================================================================

5.1 Overview
-------------
NexaFlow's built-in AI features are available on Professional and
Enterprise plans. They include:

- Text Generation: Generate emails, reports, summaries, and content
- Data Extraction: Extract structured data from unstructured text
- Classification: Categorize text, emails, or records automatically
- Sentiment Analysis: Detect tone and sentiment in customer messages
- Translation: Translate content between 50+ languages

5.2 Using AI Steps
-------------------
To add an AI step to your workflow:
1. Click "Add Action" in your workflow editor
2. Select the "AI" category
3. Choose the AI action type
4. Configure your prompt and input/output fields
5. Test the step with sample data

AI steps consume task executions at a higher rate: 5 executions
per AI step call due to the additional processing involved.

5.3 AI Accuracy and Limitations
---------------------------------
AI outputs are probabilistic and may occasionally be incorrect.
We recommend always validating AI outputs before using them in
critical business processes. NexaFlow provides confidence scores
for classification and extraction tasks to help you set
appropriate thresholds.

SECTION 6: SECURITY AND COMPLIANCE
================================================================

6.1 Data Security
------------------
NexaFlow uses AES-256 encryption for all data at rest and TLS 1.3
for all data in transit. Our infrastructure is hosted on AWS in
US-East-1 and EU-West-1 regions.

6.2 Compliance
---------------
NexaFlow is compliant with:
- SOC 2 Type II
- GDPR (for EU customers)
- CCPA (for California customers)
- HIPAA (Enterprise plan with BAA)

6.3 Data Residency
-------------------
Enterprise customers can choose their preferred data residency
region: United States, European Union, or Asia-Pacific.

6.4 Single Sign-On (SSO)
-------------------------
SSO is available on Enterprise plans and supports:
- SAML 2.0
- OAuth 2.0
- Active Directory / LDAP integration
